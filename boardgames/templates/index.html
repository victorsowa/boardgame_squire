Boardgamesquire

Boardgamesquire is a tool that helps you filter your boardgame collection. This will enable you to filter your collection by best, recommended and possible player counts (something that can't be done in bgg advanced search), as well as number of other filter options.

To use boardgamesquire, enter your boardgamegeek username and hit Get Collection. Games that are in your users collection with status own will be collected and displayed.
